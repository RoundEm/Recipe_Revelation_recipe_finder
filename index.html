Recipe Revelation

Do you ever find yourself wondering what meals you can prepare with specific ingredients available in your kitchen? Do you ever feel uninspired in the kitchen and just want some random ideas for tasty and popular recipes that utilize specific ingredients? If so, then you've come to the right place! Recipe Revelation, powered by the recipe aggregator website, Yummly, is a reverse recipe finder that allows you to retrieve full and delicious recipes from some of the most popular food blogs and recipe sites just by searching for one or more ingredient. Successfully returned recipes will include a minimum of all the ingredients you search for.

Keep ingredient names general

Don't combine separate ingredients into one entry

Don't search by methods of preparation or cooking technique

If you're having trouble finding a recipe check ingredient spelling or try entering less ingredients
