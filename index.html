Reverse Recipe Finder

Do you ever find yourself wondering what meals you can cook with the ingredients available in your kitchen. Do you ever feel uninspired in the kitchen and just want some random ideas for tasty recipes that utilize specific ingredients? If so then you're in luck! MySiteName, which is powered by the recipe aggregator website, Yummly, allows you to retrieve full and delicious recipes from some of the most popular food blogs and recipe sites, just by searching for one or more ingredients. Successfully returned recipes will include a minimum of all the ingredients you search for. For best results, keep ingredient names general and don't combine separate ingredients into one line.
